My favorite BLT Recipe

This is my favorite BLT recipe.

I am going to share this recipe with you!

Ingredients

  • Bacon
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Mustard
  • Bread

Preparation:

  1. Spread two slices of bread with mustard
  2. Stack bacon, lettuce, and tomato on one slice of bread and cover with the other
  3. Cut in half and enjoy!
